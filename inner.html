Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone is a fantasy film released in 2001, directed by Chris Columbus. It is based on J.K. Rowling's 1997 novel of the same name. The movie follows the young Harry Potter, who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets" is the second film in the Harry Potter series. It follows Harry's second year at Hogwarts, where he uncovers the mystery of a hidden chamber, confronts a deadly monster, and learns more about his connection to the dark wizard Voldemort.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban" is the third installment in the Harry Potter series, where Harry learns about the escape of Sirius Black from Azkaban prison. As Harry navigates his third year at Hogwarts, he uncovers dark secrets about his past, his family, and the dangerous Dementors.
